About Us

We are Service-Disabled Veterans and mental health experts committed to preventing tragedies like suicides, homicides, school shootings, terrorism, cyberbullying, and scams. Utilizing personal experiences, we've created a groundbreaking AI platform that detects and prevents crises by involving the right people and support systems before it's too late.

Suicide Prevention

NSD & Burnout

Thrive ID

THRIVE-ID is a comprehensive assessment tool designed to evaluate critical wellness factors: Trauma, Substance Abuse, Anxiety, Stress, Functionality, and Environment, with a strong emphasis on building resilience.

By focusing on inner resilience, THRIVE-ID helps individuals identify where they need human involvement to prevent crises and improve long-term well-being.

How It Works

PreCrisis.AI utilizes advanced AI algorithms to analyze real-time data, including stress levels and behavioral changes. Our platform is HIPAA and SOC 3 compliant, ensuring the highest standards of privacy and security. We offer both frontier models and local offline models suitable for various environments, including hardened or commercial systems.

Support Our Technology

Thrive ID

THRIVE-ID is a comprehensive assessment tool designed to evaluate critical wellness factors: Trauma, Substance Abuse, Anxiety, Stress, Functionality, and Environment, with a strong emphasis on building Resilience. It integrates an advanced, standardized trigger evaluation, providing pivotal insights that the AI leverages to identify and prioritize areas in need.

By focusing on inner resilience, THRIVE-ID aims to holistically identify challenges so individuals know where they specifically need human involvement to prevent crises and improve long-term well-being.

Our Origin

PreCrisis.AI was born at a Veterans Mental Health Hackathon, where our innovative approach earned us second place. Inspired by personal losses and a commitment to preventing tragedies, we set out to create a solution that could make a real difference.

LifeLine.vet: Our Foundation

LifeLine.vet is the parent company researching & pioneering AI solutions to solve the world's most pressing problems. Our mission is to enhance peace, justice, and ensure a safer, more comfortable, and resilient future for generations to come.

Operating in remote and isolated environments such as the Arctic, space, Mars, deep sea, submarines, and scientific expeditions, LifeLine.vet provides critical support where it's needed most.
